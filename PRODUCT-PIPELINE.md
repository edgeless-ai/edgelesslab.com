## Part 2: Autoreason Adversarial Scoring

### Scoring Dimensions (each 1-10)

- **D** = Demand signal (market validation from research)
- **B** = Buildability (can ship in 1-3 days from existing infra)
- **L** = Leverage (uses David's unique, hard-to-replicate experience)
- **F** = Differentiation (no good alternatives exist)
- **R** = Revenue potential (price x estimated volume)
- **S** = Content synergy (drives blog traffic, cross-sells, establishes authority)

### Author A: Initial Rankings

Weighting: D(25%) + B(20%) + L(20%) + F(15%) + R(10%) + S(10%)

### Strawman Challenge

"Most of these are me-too products. The market is flooded with AI coding guides. What actually differentiates Edgeless?"

Response: The differentiator is **production battle scars**. Most AI tool guides are written by people who read the docs. David has:
- Lost $252 to an agent (safety patterns)
- Run 8 concurrent crons on a VPS (deployment playbook)
- Built a multi-agent dispatch system (orchestration blueprint)
- Scored 105+ generative art experiments (art pipeline)
- Shipped 11 products as a solo dev (launch toolkit)

The strawman is right that generic "prompt engineering" products are commodity. The products that score highest must lean into **specific production failures and systems that actually run**.

### Blind Judge Panel (5 judges, Borda count)

**Judge 1 (Market Analyst)**: Weights demand signal and revenue potential heavily.
**Judge 2 (Builder)**: Weights buildability and existing infrastructure leverage.
**Judge 3 (Differentiation Expert)**: Weights uniqueness and moat.
**Judge 4 (Content Strategist)**: Weights blog synergy and brand building.
**Judge 5 (Customer)**: Weights "would I actually buy this?"

## Paperclip Operating Model Notes

- **Dispatch** (this session): Plans the week, scores products, writes the execution doc
- **Workers** (daily sessions): Each day, a new session builds one product + blog
- **Quality gate**: Every product must have: README, 3+ screenshots, Gumroad listing, blog post, cross-links to related products
- **Feedback loop**: Check Gumroad analytics daily, adjust pricing and ordering based on actual sales
- **No scope creep**: Each product is a self-contained deliverable. Ship it, list it, move on.
- **Blog-first marketing**: No paid ads. SEO + social sharing from genuinely useful blog posts.
