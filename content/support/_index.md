## App Support

Questions, bug reports, or feature requests for any Edgeless Lab app — we'd love to hear from you.

**Email: [help@edgelesslab.com](mailto:help@edgelesslab.com)**

We typically respond within 1–2 business days.

### The Clapper

Hands-free camera control — clap or snap to record video, take photos, flip the camera, and more. All sound detection happens entirely on your device: no audio is ever recorded, stored, or transmitted, and the app collects no data.

Common questions:

- **Detection feels too sensitive / not sensitive enough** — adjust the Sensitivity slider in Settings.
- **Gestures do the wrong thing** — every gesture's action is configurable in Settings → Gesture Mappings.
- **Microphone or camera access** — The Clapper needs mic access to hear claps and camera access to record. You can change these anytime in iOS Settings → The Clapper.
- **Background listening** — off by default. The mic is released whenever you leave the app unless you enable Background Listening in Settings.
