# Digital Product Launch Toolkit

A complete system for packaging, pricing, and shipping digital products on Gumroad.

## What's Inside

- **guide.md** -- The full guide (~4,500 words). Covers everything from deciding what to package through post-launch monitoring.
- **templates/** -- Ready-to-use templates for listings, READMEs, pricing decisions, launch checklists, and wave planning.
- **scripts/** -- A link verifier (shell) and a cover image generator (Python/Pillow).
- **examples/** -- A worked product graph showing cross-sell architecture across 7 products.

## Who This Is For

Solo developers, indie hackers, and creators who want to turn their knowledge into products people actually buy. Specifically:

- You have built things but never sold them
- You have sold one or two things but want a repeatable process
- You keep starting projects that never ship

This toolkit assumes you already know *something* worth packaging. It does not teach you a skill. It teaches you how to sell the skills you already have.

## Quick Start

1. Read `guide.md` cover to cover (25 min)
2. Fill out `templates/pricing-worksheet.md` for your first product
3. Use `templates/listing-template.md` to write your Gumroad listing
4. Run through `templates/launch-checklist.md` before you publish
5. Plan your next 2 products with `templates/wave-planner.md`
