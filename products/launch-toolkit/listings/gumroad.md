# Gumroad Listing: Digital Product Launch Toolkit

## Short Description
Templates, checklists, and scripts for shipping digital products on Gumroad — from someone who's launched 17 and counting.

## Full Description

### The pain
You built something useful. Now what? The gap between "working code" and "product someone pays for" is listing copy, pricing, packaging, and launch sequencing. Most developers skip this and wonder why nobody buys their $5 GitHub repo with a README that says "TODO."

### The credential
Edgeless Labs has shipped 17 digital products on Gumroad across AI tools, generative art, and developer infrastructure. This toolkit is the exact process used to go from "I have some code" to "people are buying this." Including the mistakes.

### What's inside
- **Listing template** — the pain-credential-contents formula that converts browsers to buyers. Fill in the blanks, paste into Gumroad.
- **README template** — < 5 minute setup format that sets expectations and reduces support questions
- **Launch checklist** — 20 items to verify before publishing (the one about dead links exists because we shipped one)
- **Pricing worksheet** — structured decision framework for the $9-$39 sweet spot
- **Wave planner** — template for sequencing multiple product launches with narrative threads
- **Link verification script** — checks your Gumroad URLs return 200 before you publish
- **Cover image generator** — Python script that produces clean 1280x1280 covers
- **The full guide** (~4,500 words) — pricing psychology, cross-sell architecture, the content flywheel, common mistakes, post-launch monitoring
