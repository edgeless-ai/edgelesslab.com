Blinkless

Fixes closed eyes in group photos. Pick 2–10 shots of the same moment — Blinkless finds the best take of every face, eyes open, and merges them into one photo. Real faces from your own photos, never AI-generated.

01 — Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 2026. This policy applies to the Blinkless iOS app by Edgeless Lab LLC.

Blinkless is built to keep your photos yours:

  • All processing happens on-device, using Apple's Neural Engine. Your photos never leave your phone.
  • Nothing is uploaded to any server. The app performs no network transfer of your photos or any data derived from them.
  • No accounts, no sign-up, no tracking, no analytics, no third-party SDKs.
  • No data is collected. The App Store privacy label for Blinkless is "Data Not Collected."
  • Your originals are never modified. Merged results are saved as new photos in your library.

Questions about this policy? Email help@edgelesslab.com.
