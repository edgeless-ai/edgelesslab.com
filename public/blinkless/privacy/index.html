Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 2026. This privacy policy applies to the Blinkless iOS app by Edgeless Lab LLC.

Blinkless fixes closed eyes in group photos: you pick 2–10 shots of the same moment, and it finds the best take of every face (eyes open) and merges them into one photo. It uses real faces from your own photos — never AI-generated faces.

  • All processing happens on-device, using Apple's Neural Engine. Your photos never leave your phone.
  • Nothing is uploaded to any server. The app performs no network transfer of your photos or any data derived from them.
  • No accounts, no sign-up, no tracking, no analytics, no third-party SDKs.
  • No data is collected. The App Store privacy label for Blinkless is "Data Not Collected."
  • Your originals are never modified. Merged results are saved as new photos in your library.

Because Blinkless collects no data, there is nothing for us to sell, share, retain, or delete. If you have any questions about this policy, email help@edgelesslab.com.
