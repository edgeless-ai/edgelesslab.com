Fryer Energy Balance — FR-101
Heat Duty Calculation — Step by Step
① Sensible Heat — Chip Heating
Formula: Q = ṁ · Cₚ · ΔT ṁ chips: 258.7 lb/hr (wet chips entering fryer) Cₚ masa: 0.45 BTU/(lb·°F) Perry's Chemical Engineers' Handbook, food solids ΔT: 350 − 75 = 275 °F (75°F ambient → 350°F fryer bath temp) Q_sensible: 258.7 × 0.45 × 275 Q_sensible = 32,019 BTU/hr
② Latent Heat — Water Evaporation (dominant)
Water evap.: 103.7 lb/hr Chips enter at 52.4% moisture → exit at 1.5% moisture h_fg at 212°F: 970.3 BTU/lb Perry's Table 2-305 (steam tables) Q_latent: 103.7 × 970.3 Q_latent = 100,620 BTU/hr
③ Oil Heating Load (maintain bath temp)
Method: 15% of Q_process Empirical allowance for oil circulation thermal losses Q_oil: 0.15 × (32,019 + 100,620) Q_oil = 19,892 BTU/hr → ~23,500 BTU/hr (rounded)

Total Fryer Heat Duty
Q_sensible: 32,019 BTU/hr Q_latent: 100,620 BTU/hr Q_oil: 23,500 BTU/hr
